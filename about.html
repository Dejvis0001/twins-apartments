About Us


Twins Apartments is your perfect getaway in Ksamil, Albania. Located near the stunning Saranda Bay Beach, our recently renovated property offers modern comfort and luxury for families, couples, and solo travelers.
Our spacious apartments feature complimentary WiFi, private parking, and full-day security. Enjoy sea views from your terrace, relax on the balcony with outdoor furniture, and cook in a well-equipped kitchen. We provide allergy-free, non-smoking accommodations for a healthy stay.
