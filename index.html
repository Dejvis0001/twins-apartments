SeaSide Apartaments

It's not Perfect... It's Paradise

Located in the heart of Ksamil, just steps from Saranda Bay Beach and Ksamil Main Beach, Twins Apartments offers a peaceful retreat. Recently renovated, our property features a beautiful garden, shared lounge, and complimentary WiFi. Private parking is also available on site.

Our apartment includes a terrace with stunning sea views, two bedrooms, a living room with a flat-screen TV, and a fully equipped kitchen. With two bathrooms and a balcony overlooking the garden, your comfort is assured. Enjoy an allergy-free, non-smoking environment for a relaxing stay. Welcome to Twins Apartments – your perfect getaway in Ksamil.
